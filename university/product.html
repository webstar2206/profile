Services and Products Offered by Banasthali Vidyapith

Welcome to Banasthali Vidyapith's comprehensive range of services and products aimed at fostering holistic development and academic excellence. Our commitment to providing top-notch education and support services is reflected in the offerings below:

Academic Programs

Undergraduate Programs
  • Bachelor of Arts (BA): Explore a diverse range of subjects including humanities, social sciences, and fine arts.
  • Bachelor of Science (BSc): Dive into the world of science with specialized majors in physics, chemistry, biology, and more.
  • Bachelor of Technology (BTech): Equip yourself with cutting-edge skills in engineering disciplines like computer science, electronics, mechanical, and civil engineering.
Postgraduate Programs
  • Master of Arts (MA): Deepen your understanding in disciplines such as psychology, sociology, economics, and political science.
  • Master of Science (MSc): Specialize in areas like mathematics, environmental science, biotechnology, and more.
  • Master of Technology (MTech): Advance your expertise in engineering fields through specialized coursework and research opportunities.

Academic Support Services

Career Counseling
  • Personalized guidance to help students identify their strengths, interests, and career goals.
  • Assistance in choosing the right academic path and career trajectory.
Library Services
  • Access to a vast collection of academic resources, including books, journals, and digital databases.
  • Dedicated librarians available to assist with research and reference inquiries.
Tutoring and Mentoring
  • Individualized support from experienced faculty members to enhance academic performance and skill development.
  • Peer mentoring programs to facilitate collaborative learning and knowledge sharing.

Extracurricular Activities

Sports Facilities
  • State-of-the-art sports infrastructure for various indoor and outdoor activities, including athletics, cricket, basketball, and swimming.
  • Coaching and training programs conducted by qualified instructors to nurture sporting talent.
Cultural Events and Clubs
  • Opportunities to participate in cultural festivals, music concerts, dance performances, and theatrical productions.
  • Student-led clubs and societies catering to diverse interests such as photography, literature, debating, and environmental awareness.
Community Outreach Programs
  • Engage in meaningful social service initiatives aimed at making a positive impact on the local community.
  • Volunteer opportunities with NGOs, government agencies, and community development projects.

At Banasthali Vidyapith, we strive to offer a comprehensive educational experience that goes beyond the traditional classroom setting. Explore our range of services and products to discover how we can support your academic and personal growth journey.
